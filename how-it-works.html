II

Coverage Scoring

Rewarding variety over repetition

Scoring Detail

Train the Whole Body

Coverage rewards you for training a variety of body parts rather than repeating the same muscles every session. It has three sub-components.

Group Coverage
UP TO 70 PTS

Train as many of the 7 major muscle groups as possible each week. More groups covered means more points earned.

Double Hit
UP TO 50 PTS

Train muscle groups on two or more separate days. Hitting a body part twice shows serious commitment to growth.

Cardio Sessions
UP TO 30 PTS

Regular cardio sessions throughout the week. Running, cycling, rowing, or any cardio activity counts toward this target.

Arms includes biceps and triceps. Posterior chain includes hamstrings and glutes. Full-body exercises count toward all seven groups.

Personalized to You

Training frequency — if you train 2 days/week, you need to hit fewer groups than someone training 5+ days.

Age — targets are slightly reduced for users over 50 to reflect realistic training volume.

Sex — female users get a slight shift toward cardio weighting in the sub-component balance.

Hardware Integration

Real-Time Heart Rate

Connect any Bluetooth heart rate monitor for live tracking during workouts. Your heart rate data powers calorie calculations, intensity scoring, and ghost comparisons.

Any BLE Monitor

Works with any Bluetooth heart rate monitor that broadcasts the standard Heart Rate Service—Polar, Wahoo, Garmin, Coospo, and more.

Background Capture

Heart rate keeps recording when you lock your phone or switch apps. A native foreground service maintains the BLE connection so you never miss a beat.

Live HR Chart

A real-time heart rate chart scrolls during your workout, showing your current BPM alongside your ghost's heart rate for visual comparison.

Accurate Calories

With a connected HR monitor, calories use the Keytel (2005) formula—factoring heart rate, age, sex, and weight for research-backed accuracy.

Your Data

Privacy First

Your workout data lives on your device. No ads, no data sales, no uploads unless you choose to share.

Local Storage

Workouts, heart rate, GPS tracks, scores, and body composition are stored in an on-device database. Nothing leaves your phone unless you choose to share or export it.

Backup & Restore

Manual and automatic backups save your entire database as a JSON file. Restore from any backup to recover your data on a new device.

Optional Social

Social features are opt-in. Sign in with a magic link to add friends, share workouts, and challenge each other. Only workout summaries are shared—never raw heart rate or GPS data.
